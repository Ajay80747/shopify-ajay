Frequently Asked Questions

What is ShopMix?
ShopMix is an online platform offering a wide range of products including electronics, fashion, home appliances and more — all at amazing discounts!
How can I place an order?
Simply browse through the products, click "Add to Cart", then proceed to checkout to place your order securely.
What are the payment options?
We accept Razorpay, UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking and Cash on Delivery (COD in select areas).
How do I track my order?
After logging in, go to the "Orders" section to view real-time status and details of your purchases.
What is the return/refund policy?
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return on most products. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days after pickup and verification.
